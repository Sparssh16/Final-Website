About Shri Ram Food Products

Shri Ram Food Products takes pride in offering traditionally prepared Murabba varieties that have been cherished for generations. What makes us unique is our commitment to quality and health, as our business is run by a doctor with a strong emphasis on hygiene and the use of natural ingredients.

Our production process maintains the highest standards of cleanliness and safety, ensuring that every jar of Murabba not only delights your taste buds but also provides natural nutrients your body needs.

We began as a small family business in Rohtak and have grown through word-of-mouth and the loyalty of customers who value our authentic preparation methods and affordable prices. We offer options for purchasing even in small quantities to make our products accessible to everyone.
